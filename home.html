Creative Budgeting Toolkit

The Creative Budgeting Toolkit is a futuristic finance tool designed for modern creators — whether you're an independent musician, digital influencer, filmmaker, freelance artist, or actor. We know managing money in a non-traditional, project-based career is tough — so we built something that’s actually *built for you*.

Track income from gigs, brand deals, sessions, and royalties. Visualize your monthly and yearly expenses with clean dashboards. Plan your savings goals, manage recurring costs, and stay financially organized across your chaotic creative journey — all in a sleek, intuitive dashboard.

About Us

We’re a youth-led initiative focused on empowering creatives through accessible financial tools. Founded by Jude Praveen, a student and aspiring brand strategist, this toolkit reflects a mission to make money management not just manageable, but inspiring. Our vision is to combine art, tech, and finance into tools that speak your language — clear, beautiful, and effective.
